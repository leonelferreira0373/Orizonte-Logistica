Our Logistics Solutions

End-to-end supply chain management for Angola and beyond.

Ocean Freight (FCL & LCL)

Comprehensive sea cargo solutions. We handle Full Container Loads (FCL) and Less than Container Loads (LCL) from Portugal, USA, and Brazil directly to Luanda and Lobito ports.

  • Weekly consolidations from Lisbon
  • Heavy machinery transport

Air Cargo & Express

When time is critical, our air freight services ensure rapid delivery to Quatro de Fevereiro International Airport. Ideal for documents, perishables, and urgent spare parts.

  • Daily flights from TAP/TAAG hubs
  • Door-to-Airport & Door-to-Door

Customs Brokerage

Navigating Angolan customs (AGT) can be complex. Our licensed despachantes handle all documentation, duties, and taxes to prevent delays at the border.

  • DU (Documento Único) processing
  • Tariff classification experts

Procurement Assistance

Need to buy goods in the USA or Portugal but don't have a local address? We purchase on your behalf, consolidate the items at our warehouses, and ship them to Angola.

  • Personal Shopper Service
  • B2B Sourcing

Inland Transport

Delivery across all 18 provinces. From Luanda to Huambo, Benguela, or Cabinda, our fleet ensures your cargo reaches its final destination safely.

Oil & Gas Logistics

Specialized handling for the energy sector. We manage the supply chain from Houston (USA) to offshore rigs and onshore bases in Angola.
